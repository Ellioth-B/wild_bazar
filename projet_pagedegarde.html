About us

Wildware was founded in 2019 by 3 developers and 3 commercials, with the aim of selling connected items.     The name of the company means "Wild Article", referring to the school where the 6 people met.     Wildware is a young start-up specializing in the marketing of the "Headset", a headset for receiving and making calls, as well as listening to music online with a voice command, without any visual interference.Currently, the products sold are expanding in many new ranges around connected objects.
